As well as being the scientific name, iris is also widely used as a common name for all Iris species, as well as some belonging to other closely related genera. It is a popular garden flower. Based on the fact that plant expert often decides the species of Iris based on the size of their petal and sepal, it uses width and length of petal and sepal to do the visualization and have a better insight of Iris species and its petal and sepal.Three species: setosa, versicolor, and virginica, are used in this information visualization page.
